People in the street

Passersby are usually simply background; however, once I focus on them and imbue them with narratives, they no longer remain as mere background but become people with individual meanings, creating a relation.

This work is a story book consisting of photos of pedestrians that are captured from Google Street View. I created fictional narratives around their actions and thoughts based on the information given only through these limited images. This kind of projection is not much different from the ways in which we often judge others at will.
