Chronological Printer

When reading a word, most people recognize it in its whole configuration, rather than the individual alphabet or characters of which it is composed. “Chronological Printer” is a visual representation of the passage of time contained in words and writes the elements sequentially using music box system. In this process, the alphabet is spatially separated, allowing the reader to focus more closely on the individual letters that make up each word.
