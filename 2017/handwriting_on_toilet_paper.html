Handwriting on Toliet Paper

Our eyes miss a lot of information. As we are bombarded by an abundant amount of information, there is much that is forgotten and not even recognized. By inscribing detailed notation onto fragile and easily wasted toilet paper, I created this work that evokes information that is easily damaged and dismissed.
