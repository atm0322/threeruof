Wakebox

This is a club installed in the elevator of a company building, which can be accessed and enjoyed as easily as going to the restroom. When you tired or bored at work, ‘Wakebox’ would provide enjoyable respite in your busy day and change your mood for a moment, and also increase efficiency.

Wakebox Tracker

This is an app that accompanies the 'Wakebox'. It indicates the available elevator with regards to the different types of card.
